A masks and respirators production project named Baltic Masks was founded during the peak of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020. The company saw a gap in the market for high-quality, affordable masks and quickly established itself as a leading producer of personal protective equipment . With a mission to keep communities safe and healthy, Baltic masks continues to thrive as demand for masks remains high even as the world begins to recover from the pandemic.

Baltics masks key facts

Customers and distributors network with constant cash flow

Reliable supply and distribution chains

Experienced engineers and managers teams

Protected trademarks

Certified products with licenses

15mln € of investments in 2021 only

2000m2 of production and 16 production lines
